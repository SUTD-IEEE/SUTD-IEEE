I'm the Best Coder! Challenge 2019

Calling all CODERS!

Following the success of the National Data Science Challenge 2019, the LARGEST data analytics competition in Singapore is now coming your way! Proudly organised by Shopee, I’m the Best Coder! Challenge 2019 is now open for registration.

This challenge is free, open to all levels of experience and all nationalities!

To join, simply follow these 3 steps:
  1. Gather 2 - 4 team members to form a team
  2. Select one of these 3 categories to compete in: Pre-Tertiary, Undergraduates and Open
  3. Prepare yourself for the challenge in Mid-October 2019!
    (Note that you must be physically present in Singapore on the day of the competition, which will be confirmed by August 2019)
The top 3 teams from each category will walk away with a pooled cash prize of SGD 18,000, and…guess what!

Return tickets to visit Shopee Indonesia will also be awarded to the champions from each category!

Winning teams will be invited for an interview to discuss potential full-time or internship opportunities with Shopee.

Stop waiting! Sign up now at https://careers.shopee.sg/bestcoder!
